Student Information Management

Student Information Management refers to the systematic handling of student-related data, encompassing a wide range of information such as personal details, academic records, attendance etc.

Teacher Management

Teacher Management involves the administration and coordination of all aspects related to the teaching staff within an educational institution. Maintaining detailed profiles for each teacher, including personal information, qualifications, certifications.

Attendance and Behavior Management

Attendance and Behavior Management involves the systematic tracking and handling of student attendance and behavior in educational settings.Monitoring and recording student presence etc.

Grade Management and Reporting

The system that a school has developed to guide how teachers assess and grade student work.

Parent Portal

the online communication system through which the School can provide information to Parents.

Resource Management

When it comes to education, resource management involves making a strategic plan for organizing and using school resources. Resource management involves evaluation and maximization of such resources.

Best Application For Your Kids

SCHOOLMANAGER APP is a school information management system created and managed by KUDOS TECHNOLOGY. That APP provides an innovative and complete solution to streamline information of all activities in an educational institutions. It is a cloud based and self host education services solution that opens a universe of opportunities to automate the administrative workings of schools.

  • KUDOS SCHOOLMANAGER APP "The APP" is a value added service to educational institutions and stakeholders (school owners, teachers, parents and students).
  • The APP is an information management software that works across mobile devices; smart phones, tablets etc.
  • Parents will receive updates from time to time about upcoming events at their children’s school. The APP is compatible with Android phone only, for now.
